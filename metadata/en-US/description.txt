Skip the massive hassle it is trying to find all those great text based emojis, and have them all in one easily accessable place, a keyboard! Including everyone's favorite: ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ and over 30 more. Either use the keyboard as intended, or long-press on the emoticon to copy it to the clipboard

The app is minuscule in size, requires no internet connection, and simply works. What more can you ask for?
